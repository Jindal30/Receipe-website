Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour (maida)
  • 2 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • 2–3 tbsp milk (as needed for dough)
  • Oil or ghee for deep frying

For Sugar Syrup:

  • 1 ½ cups sugar
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 3–4 cardamom pods (crushed)
  • Few strands saffron (optional)
  • 1 tsp rose water (optional)

Steps to Make

  1. In a bowl, mix milk powder, flour, baking soda, and ghee.
  2. Add little milk gradually and make a soft, smooth dough (not sticky). Rest for 5–10 minutes.
  3. Meanwhile, make sugar syrup: boil sugar + water until slightly sticky,
    add cardamom, saffron, and rose water. Keep warm.
  4. Shape the dough into small smooth balls (no cracks).
  5. Heat oil/ghee on low-medium flame. Fry balls gently until golden brown.
  6. Immediately soak the hot gulab jamuns in warm syrup for at least 2 hours.
  7. Serve warm or chilled, garnished with pistachios or rose petals.

Barfi

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk powder
  • 1 cup condensed milk
  • 2 tbsp ghee (clarified butter)
  • ½ cup milk
  • ½ tsp cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts (pistachios, almonds) for garnish

Steps to Make

  1. Grease a tray/plate with ghee and keep aside.
  2. In a pan, heat ghee and add milk + condensed milk. Stir well.
  3. Add milk powder slowly and mix to form a smooth lump-free mixture.
  4. Cook on low flame until mixture thickens and starts leaving the sides of the pan.
  5. Add cardamom powder and mix.
  6. Transfer mixture to greased tray and spread evenly.
  7. Garnish with chopped nuts and press lightly.
  8. Let it cool for 1–2 hours, then cut into square/diamond-shaped pieces.
